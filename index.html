The Concept | 引言

A personal notebook opens into a collective sleep archive.

I spent weeks tracking my own sleep patterns, waking up to jot down fading emotions before they dissolved into the morning light. This intimate, personal ritual sparked a profound curiosity: What does a dream actually look like when translated into raw, neurological data? To answer this, my personal diary merges with the Dream Emotion Evaluation Dataset (DEED). It captures the electrical echoes of 38 human subjects during their REM (Rapid Eye Movement) sleep phase. Here, abstract biological signals and deeply private reveries are rendered into a navigable "Consciousness Helix"—a physical, three-dimensional space where you can explore the galaxy of dreams.

我曾连续数周追踪自己的睡眠轨迹，在晨光吞噬梦境前，匆忙记下残存的情绪。这种极度私密的自我量化实践引发了一个深刻的好奇：当梦境被转化为原始的神经学数据时，它究竟是什么形状？为了寻找答案，我的个人记录与 DEED（梦境情感评估数据集）相交汇，捕捉了 38 位受试者在 REM（快速眼动）睡眠阶段的神经电波回声。在这里，抽象的生物信号与极其私密的幻想被渲染成一个可漫游的“意识螺旋”——一个可以探索梦境的三维星系。

Chromatic Emotion | 色彩情绪 Dream State / 梦境状态

Colors are not random; they are the pigments of the soul. Golden hues radiate warmth and positive sentiments; deep blues and violets signify negative descents into sadness; while crimson pulses alert to underlying fears. It is a spectrum mapping the emotional tone of the dream.

颜色并非随机，它们是灵魂的颜料。金色散发温暖与积极的共鸣；蓝紫色象征着坠入悲伤的消极下沉；而深红色脉冲则在展示了内心深处的恐惧。这是精准映射梦境情感基调的光谱。
